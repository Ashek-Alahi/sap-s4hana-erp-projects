# ERP KPI Catalog

## FI/CO
| KPI | Business Meaning | Portfolio Use |
|---|---|---|
| Open A/R | Customer receivables not cleared | Evaluate collection exposure |
| Open A/P | Vendor liabilities not cleared | Evaluate payment obligations |
| Vendor balance | Vendor subledger balance | Support payment and reconciliation review |
| Customer balance | Customer subledger balance | Support cash collection review |
| Cost center actuals | Actual expenses by cost center | Support management accounting review |
| Allocation amount | Costs distributed through assessment cycles | Explain internal cost allocation control |

## MM
| KPI | Business Meaning | Portfolio Use |
|---|---|---|
| PO-to-GR cycle | Time from purchase order to goods receipt | Monitor procurement execution |
| Three-way match exception | Difference between PO, GR, and invoice | Identify payment and receiving issues |
| Vendor price variance | Difference between expected and invoiced price | Support sourcing and invoice review |
| Duplicate PO risk | Repeated purchasing for same vendor/material | Identify inventory and payment control risk |
| GR/IR reconciliation | Goods receipt and invoice receipt clearing | Support month-end procurement accounting |

## PP
| KPI | Business Meaning | Portfolio Use |
|---|---|---|
| Planned vs produced quantity | Difference between planned and actual output | Measure production variance |
| Production order completion | Whether order reached confirmation and receipt | Track execution completion |
| Component shortage | Missing component quantity for production | Explain MRP and availability risk |
| Finished goods receipt | Quantity received into finished goods stock | Validate production inventory movement |
| MRP exception | Planning issue requiring review | Support production planning control |

## ABAP
| KPI | Business Meaning | Portfolio Use |
|---|---|---|
| Report filter coverage | Completeness of company code, account, and date filters | Validate report usability |
| Cleared item visibility | Ability to see cleared A/R or A/P documents | Support accounting review |
| Customer/vendor drilldown | Ability to move from account to line-item detail | Support user investigation |

## HCM
| KPI | Business Meaning | Portfolio Use |
|---|---|---|
| Position coverage | Positions assigned to organizational units | Review OM completeness |
| Qualification coverage | Qualifications created and assigned | Review capability documentation |
| Hiring action completion | Personnel action completed with required data | Review PA process completion |
