## Interview Story Bank

### Story 1 — FI/MM Integration

**Situation:** Procurement activities must correctly impact accounting records.  
**Task:** Execute a P2P process and understand the financial impact.  
**Action:** Created vendor/material master data, processed RFQ and PO, posted goods receipt, verified invoice, and completed payment.  
**Result:** Demonstrated how procurement execution connects to inventory, vendor liability, invoice verification, and payment clearing.

### Story 2 — Duplicate Purchase Order Risk

**Situation:** Inventory showed an unexpected quantity difference.  
**Task:** Investigate why stock exceeded the expected amount.  
**Action:** Reviewed the procurement process and identified that a duplicate PO caused excess unrestricted stock.  
**Result:** Demonstrated practical understanding of how transaction errors affect inventory valuation and operational reporting.

### Story 3 — Custom A/R Reporting

**Situation:** Standard reports may not provide the exact filter combination business users need.  
**Task:** Build a report for cleared customer accounting items.  
**Action:** Used SAP financial and customer master tables with ABAP selection options and ALV output.  
**Result:** Created a more flexible reporting approach for customer-level financial review.

### Story 4 — Production Execution

**Situation:** Manufacturing requires planning, component availability, production execution, and finished goods receipt.  
**Task:** Execute a production planning scenario from requirements to inventory update.  
**Action:** Reviewed material planning, BOM, routing, MRP, production order, goods issue, confirmation, and goods receipt.  
**Result:** Demonstrated end-to-end understanding of production operations in SAP.
